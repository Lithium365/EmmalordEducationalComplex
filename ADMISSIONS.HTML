Admission Requirements

Thank you for considering Emmalord Educational Complex for your child's education. To ensure a smooth admission process, please take note of the following requirements:

Requirements

  • Completed Admission Application Form
  • Birth Certificate of the Student
  • Passport-sized Photographs (2 copies)
  • Immunization Records
  • Previous School Records (if applicable)
  • Parent or Guardian Identification
  • Admission Fee Payment

Please ensure that all required documents are submitted accurately and promptly to facilitate the admission process. For further inquiries, feel free to contact our admissions office.

Termly Requirements

At the beginning of every term, all students are required to fulfill the following obligations:

  • Payment of School Fees
  • Submission of Medical Health Report
  • Updated Contact Information
  • Participation in Parent-Teacher Meetings

Your cooperation in adhering to these requirements is crucial for the smooth running of our academic programs. Thank you for choosing Emmalord Educational Complex.
